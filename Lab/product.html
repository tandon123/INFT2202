3 Things we like are:

The physiology of money

By Morgan Housel

Morgan Housel teaches you how to make better financial decisions and to have a healthier connection with money in his book The Psychology of Money. Rather than portraying people as ROI-maximizing machines, he demonstrates how psychology can both work in your favour and against you.

The Best Movie

Avengers Endgame

Marvel movies never let us down when we were kids, and this one is astounding and above expectations. There is just one question to ask which movie theatre we should buy tickets for whenever a new Marvel film is released.

The Best TV show Ever

Tarak Mehta Ka Oolta Chasma(TMKOC)

The show is centred on the diverse members of the Gokuldham Co-operative Housing Society, which is an apartment complex located in Powder Gali, Film City Road, Goregaon East, Mumbai.People in a society try to foster unity amidst diversity by living as a family and supporting one another during difficult times. Gokuldham's members plan several events and observe all the festivals.
